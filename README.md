## About the Datapack
This datapack is solely meant for adding Iron Spells & Spellbooks loot in Ice & Fire chests so that it's actually worth looting. The datapack is not difficult to make at all, therefor I do not wish to receive any praise for it, it was purely made for myself to begin with.
